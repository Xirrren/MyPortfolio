獨立開發 / 2D卷軸平台跳躍

Independently developed / 2D scrolling platformer


這是我第一次開發到的2D卷軸平台跳躍類遊戲，也是一堂課的期末作業，在開發中我學到很多，尤其是相機Cinemachine及動畫的運用。

This is my first time developing a 2D side-scrolling platformer game, which is also a final project for one of my courses. During the development process, I learned a lot, especially about using Cinemachine for camera control and implementing animations.

操作方式:
用AD移動Space跳躍
前往傳送石即可前往下一關

Controls:
Use A and D to move, and Space to jump.
Reach the teleport stone to proceed to the next level.

Independent Development / VR Ball Dropr


這是我第一次接觸 VR 遊戲開發，從 3D UI 設計到大型場景搭建都花了不少時間學習與實作。開發過程中，印象最深刻的部分包括如何透過程式實現球的投擲功能，以及遇到場景素材材質丟失時的處理方式。

This was my first experience with VR game development, from designing 3D UI to building large-scale scenes, which took considerable time to learn and implement. The most memorable aspects of the process were programming the ball-throwing mechanics and handling situations where scene assets lost their materials.

Device Used:
Meta Quest
Throw the ball into the designated area within the time limit to score points.


泡泡派對 BubbleParty!

BubbleParty!

團隊開發 / 2D雙人競爭地盤 / GGJ

Team Development / 2D Two-Player Battlegrounds / GGJ


這是我第一次挑戰在3天內總共5個人做出一款遊戲，主題是「泡泡」。我負責場景美術和一些 UI 轉場動畫。雖然時間很緊，但也正因為這樣，我學到怎麼快速把想法做出來，也體驗到短時間內團隊一起完成作品的感覺。

This was my first time joining a five-person team to create a game in just three days, with the theme “Bubbles.” I was in charge of environment art and some UI transition animations. Even though the time was tight, I learned how to quickly turn ideas into reality and really felt the excitement of working together to finish a project in such a short time.

操作方式:
玩家1（移動）：WASD
玩家2（移動）：IJKL
這是一款雙人對戰遊戲，玩家可選擇成為強大的污垢終結者，或是挑戰清潔極限的髒亂之王。無論你化身哪一方，都能在這場緊張刺激的對決中一較高下。準備迎戰，看看誰能掌控這場清潔大戰！

Controls:
Player 1 (Movement) : WASD
Player 2 (Movement) : IJKL
This is a two-player battle game where players can choose to become the mighty Dirt Eliminator or the ultimate King of Chaos who challenges the limits of cleanliness. No matter which side you take, you can compete in this intense and exciting showdown. Get ready to face off and see who can dominate the Cleanliness War!


光是送餐都來不及了

We can't even keep up with the food delivery schedule.

團隊開發 / 2D雙人競爭 / FGJ

Team Development / 2D Two-Player Competition / FGJ


這是我第二次參與短時間內的遊戲製作，主題是「vibe」。這次挑戰擔任程式設計，主要負責遊戲中送餐系統的實作。由於是第一次在有限時間內開發程式，過程中必須快速理清邏輯與層級關係，雖然一度手忙腳亂，但也讓我更了解如何在壓力下保持思考並完成功能。

This was my second time participating in a short-term game project with the theme “vibe.” I took on the challenge of programming and was mainly responsible for implementing the food delivery system. Since it was my first time coding under a tight deadline, I had to quickly organize the logic and hierarchy. Although it was a bit hectic at times, it helped me learn how to stay composed and complete features under pressure.

操作方式:
玩家1（移動）：WASD (衝刺):空白鍵
玩家2（移動）：上下左右 (衝刺):Control鍵
兩位玩家可以互相爭奪餐點，如果撞到對方玩家可以偷對方拿到的餐點，時間內獲得送餐次數最多的玩家獲勝。

Controls:
Player 1 (Move): WASD (Sprint): Spacebar
Player 2 (Move): Up, Down, Left, Right (Sprint): Control key
The two players can compete for food. If they bump into the other player, they can steal the food the other player has. The player who delivers the most food within the time limit wins.
